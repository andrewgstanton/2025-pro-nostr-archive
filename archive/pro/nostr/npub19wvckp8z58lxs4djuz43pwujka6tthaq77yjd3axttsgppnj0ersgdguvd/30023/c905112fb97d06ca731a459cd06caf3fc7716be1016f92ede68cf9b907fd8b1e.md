This index establishes the **canonical chronological structure**, with **ARC 1 as the beginning** and **ARC 9 as the latest point in the timeline**.

Each Chronicle is assigned:

- **ARC** ‚Üí the era in the universe timeline  
- **SEQ (letter)** ‚Üí the sequence within that era  
- **SUB (decimal)** ‚Üí optional insertions between stories  

Example:  
**2.C.0** = Arc 2, sequence C, no subsequence.  
**2.C.1** = a future Chronicle placed between C and D.

This system allows the universe to expand indefinitely without breaking continuity.
