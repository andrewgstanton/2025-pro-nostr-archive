# Remembering Charlie Kirk

Twenty-four years after the towers fell, we find ourselves remembering Sept 11 in a new way. The cost of fear, the temptation of surveillance, and now the loss of Charlie Kirk — a voice for dialogue and conviction — weigh heavily on this anniversary. Today’s set is dedicated to freedom, sovereignty, and the courage to resist trading liberty for control.
